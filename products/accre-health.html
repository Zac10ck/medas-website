AccreHealth is a comprehensive healthcare quality management and accreditation software developed by MEDAS Solutions. It digitalizes accreditation preparedness and quality management systems, enabling healthcare organizations to manage complex operational processes on a unified digital platform.

AccreHealth supports major international and regional accreditation standards including JCI (Joint Commission International), NABH (National Accreditation Board for Hospitals), NABL (National Accreditation Board for Laboratories), CBAHI (Saudi Arabia), and other regional healthcare quality standards.

AccreHealth includes 8 core modules: Document Management System, Privilege Manager, Incident Manager, KPI Module, OPPE Module (Ongoing Professional Practice Evaluation), Quality Audit Management, Patient Feedback & Complaints Management, and Infection Control Surveillance Module.

Yes, AccreHealth is ISO 45001 and ISO 20000-1:2018 certified, demonstrating our commitment to occupational health and safety management as well as IT service management excellence.
