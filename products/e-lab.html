    Frequently Asked Questions

    Common questions about E-Lab.

    E-Lab provides comprehensive sample tracking through barcode technology. From the moment a sample is collected, it's assigned a unique barcode that tracks its journey through registration, processing, testing, and result delivery. The system maintains complete chain of custody documentation, tracks sample location in real-time, manages sample routing to appropriate departments, and provides alerts for pending samples or overdue tests.
    Yes, E-Lab supports bi-directional interfaces with most major laboratory analyzers and instruments. The system can automatically receive test orders, send them to connected instruments, and capture results directly from the machines. This eliminates manual data entry, reduces errors, and significantly speeds up the result delivery process. We support instruments from leading manufacturers including Roche, Siemens, Abbott, Beckman Coulter, and many others.
    E-Lab includes comprehensive quality control management with automated QC result tracking, Levey-Jennings charts, Westgard rules implementation, out-of-range alerts, and validation workflows. The system ensures all results meet quality standards before release, maintains complete QC documentation for accreditation purposes, and provides detailed audit trails for regulatory compliance.
    Absolutely. E-Lab is designed with multi-tenant architecture to support satellite collection centers and multiple branch locations. Samples collected at remote centers can be tracked as they're transported to the central laboratory, and results are available across all locations in real-time. The system provides centralized management while allowing branch-specific configurations, pricing, and reporting.
    E-Lab includes dedicated patient and client portals. Patients can log in to view and download their reports, track test status, and book appointments online. The client portal serves referring physicians and healthcare facilities, allowing them to order tests, track samples in real-time, download results in bulk, and access historical data. Both portals are mobile-responsive and can send automated notifications when results are ready.
