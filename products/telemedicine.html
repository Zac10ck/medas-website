FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Telemedicine.

Our telemedicine platform delivers HD video quality with adaptive streaming that adjusts to available bandwidth. The system supports 720p to 1080p video resolution, ensuring clear visual communication between doctors and patients. The platform automatically optimizes video quality based on network conditions, and includes features like screen sharing for reviewing medical images or documents, and recording capabilities for documentation purposes when authorized.
Security is our top priority. All telemedicine consultations use end-to-end encryption (E2E) with AES-256 encryption standards. Patient data is protected according to healthcare privacy regulations including HIPAA and GCC health authority requirements. The platform includes secure authentication, encrypted data transmission, secure cloud storage, automatic session timeout, and complete audit trails for all access to patient information.
Yes, our telemedicine solution includes native mobile applications for both iOS and Android platforms. Patients can download the app from the App Store or Google Play, book appointments, conduct video consultations, view prescriptions, access their medical history, and make payments directly from their smartphones or tablets. The mobile apps are optimized for low-bandwidth scenarios and work reliably even with moderate internet connectivity.
Absolutely. Our telemedicine platform is designed to integrate seamlessly with MEDAS HIMS and other popular EMR systems through standard HL7 interfaces and APIs. The integration allows automatic patient data synchronization, real-time access to medical records during consultations, automatic prescription transfer to pharmacy systems, and consolidated billing. For non-MEDAS systems, we provide API documentation and integration support to ensure smooth connectivity.
The platform includes built-in redundancy and failover mechanisms. If video connection is lost, the system automatically attempts to reconnect. If the issue persists, both doctor and patient receive notifications with alternative connection options. The platform includes an audio-only fallback option, in-app chat messaging, and the ability to reschedule the appointment. Our 24/7 technical support team is available to assist with any technical issues, and the consultation session data is automatically saved to prevent data loss.
