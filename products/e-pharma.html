Comprehensive Pharmacy Management

Everything you need to run a modern pharmacy efficiently with full hospital integration.

E-Prescription

Prescriptions from consulting doctors automatically converted into dispensing units without manual intervention.

FIFO/LIFO Dispensing

Compliance with First-In-First-Out and Last-In-First-Out dispensing methods for optimal inventory rotation.
