Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about E-Veterinary.

Yes, E-Veterinary is designed to manage records for all types of animals including dogs, cats, birds, reptiles, exotic pets, livestock, and more. The system includes species-specific templates for SOAP notes, vitals tracking (with normal ranges adjusted for each species), vaccination schedules, treatment protocols, and medication dosing. You can customize fields and workflows based on the types of animals your practice treats.
E-Veterinary uses the industry-standard SOAP (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan) format for clinical documentation. The system provides structured templates that guide veterinarians through documenting patient history, physical examination findings, diagnosis, and treatment plans. Each SOAP note can include vitals, test results, images from PACS integration, medication orders, and follow-up instructions. All notes are timestamped, auditable, and accessible from the patient's complete medical history.
Yes, E-Veterinary includes integrated SMS and email communication features. The system automatically sends appointment reminders, vaccination due notifications, follow-up care instructions, and general health tips to pet owners. You can configure automated reminder schedules for wellness visits, preventive care, and medication refills. All communications are logged in the client record for reference and compliance purposes.
Absolutely. E-Veterinary supports integration with veterinary laboratory analyzers for automatic test result capture and viewing. The system also includes PACS (Picture Archiving and Communication System) integration for DICOM imaging, allowing you to store, view, and manage X-rays, ultrasounds, and other diagnostic images directly within patient records. This eliminates manual data entry and ensures all diagnostic information is centralized and easily accessible.
E-Veterinary includes comprehensive billing features designed for veterinary practices. The system handles consultation fees, diagnostic services, surgical procedures, medications, pet supplies, grooming, boarding, and more. It supports multiple payment methods, package pricing for wellness plans, discounts, insurance claims (for pet insurance), and detailed invoicing. The barcode inventory integration ensures accurate billing and automatic stock updates when products are sold.
