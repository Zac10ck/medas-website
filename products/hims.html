Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about HIMS.

HIMS is designed to support hospitals of all sizes, from small clinics with 10-20 beds to large multi-specialty hospitals with 500+ beds. The system scales seamlessly with your organization's growth and can be customized based on the number of departments, specialties, and operational modules required.
HIMS provides real-time bed availability tracking, automated bed assignment, and comprehensive admission workflows. The system manages bed reservations, transfers between wards, discharge planning, and census reporting. It also tracks bed occupancy rates and generates alerts for efficient bed utilization management.
Yes, HIMS is fully compliant with Ministry of Health (MOH) regulations across all GCC countries. It supports NABIDH (Dubai Health Authority), Malaffi (Abu Dhabi HIE), HL7 interoperability standards, ICD-10 diagnosis coding, CPT procedure coding, and electronic claims submission requirements for all major insurance providers in the region.

It can connect with lab analyzers, PACS systems, and imaging equipment to enable automated result capture and seamless data exchange across all diagnostic departments.
